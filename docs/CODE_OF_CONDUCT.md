## âš–ï¸ Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report to moderators** via:
   - Discord DM to moderators
   - Telegram direct message
   - GitHub issue (for public matters)
   - Email: [will be added]

2. **Provide details:**
   - What happened
   - When and where
   - Who was involved
   - Any evidence (screenshots, logs)

### Confidentiality

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. We will respect the confidentiality and security of the reporter.

### Consequences

Community leaders will determine appropriate consequences for any violation, which may include:

**First Offense:**
- Warning (public or private)
- Temporary mute/ban

**Second Offense:**
- Temporary ban (1-30 days)
- Removal from specific channels

**Severe or Repeated Offenses:**
- Permanent ban from all community spaces
- Report to platform administrators if necessary

### Appeal Process

If you believe you were unfairly moderated, you may appeal by:
- Contacting community administrators
- Providing your perspective
- Requesting review of the decision
